About Mullis Lawn Maintenance

Over 20 years of professional service!

attended and graduated from Palatka High. After several years of cutting grass while attending school, I founded Mullis Lawn Maintenance of Palatka in 1999. I have grown this business from a lawn maintenance business that focused on primarily residential properties, to a commercial enterprise that is capable of handling everything from small private residences to large commercial properties. As the sole owner of Mullis Lawn Maintenance, I personally perform the work on most jobs and insure that all jobs meet the requirements, and in most cases exceed requirements, of agreed upon contracts. Mullis Lawn Maintenance of Palatka, Inc. is built on dependable, affordable service. This is demonstrated by more than a dozen private clients as well as a more than a dozen commercial clients that have maintained our services for more than ten years running. Clients such as the Beck Dealerships, Norfolk Southern Railroad and Hiawatha Condominiums to name a few. The last few years have seen a growth in land clearing and maintenance of large commercial properties. This growth has allowed us to purchase equipment that is well suited for this bid as well as for clearing the retention ponds at the Airport that are currently overgrown and unable to be mowed. Should the Airport be interested, we would be happy to provide a demonstration of this service at no charge. I want to thank the City of Palatka for this opportunity to serve and look forward to answering any questions that might arrive in the future.
