About our Land Clearing Company

Founded in 1999, Mullis Lawn Maintenance has been providing exceptional service to Putnam County residents for over 20 years. Our humble beginnings combined with customer satisfaction has grown our business from a small lawn cutting service to the ever expanding business we operate today.

With bigger and better equipment, we are now able to offer land clearing services. Our residential land clearing services can take a wooded lot and selectively clear it to be the functional property you deserve. It does not stop there! We also provide commercial land clearing services to businesses, organizations, and local government properties.

Whether it be a lot that needs clearing for a future home, a business with overgrown retention ponds, or a roadside that needs maintenance, we have it covered!

Give us a call today to get a quote, big or small, on any of your outdoor needs.

OUR LAND CLEARING SERVICES

Residential Land Clearing

Need a lot cleared for a new home? Or maybe since we are in Florida, you are getting prepared for hurricane season, and those old trees are a little too close to the house? If so, then our residential land clearing services are right for you! Our team comes prepared with the skills and equipment needed, to perform jobs like brush removal, tree removal, stump removal, ditch clearing, and more.

Commercial Land Clearing

For commercial land clearing, we service small to large businesses, organizations, and local government properties. If hundreds of acres are what you are sitting on, do not be worried! You found the right company for the job. We will perform maintenance of roadside ditches, clearing land for pasture, mow commercial lots, and bush hog those fields of palmettos and small shrubs.
